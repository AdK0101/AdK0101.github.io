About Me

So, again. My name is Aditya Khan. I was born in Bangladesh but I immigrated to Canada when I was very young. As a result, I am fluent in both English and Bengali. I'd like to add to that list of languages, so I'm trying my hand at French too, with no small help from the brilliant teachers at my school. I live with my mother, my father, and my sister.

This is a picture of me at grade 8 graduation.

Throughout my life, one of the few constants has been my drive to learn. Of course, a lot of this is done through schooling. From grades 5-8, I was part of the SAGE (formerly Actal) program at Greystone Heights. It was there that I honestly think I made my best friends. I think that my time at Greystone was a lot more fun than high school (even if I'll never admit it to my elementary school friends' faces). But, high school has been fun too. I'm a part of the International Baccaulaeureate (IB) program now, and man is it a slog. But, in truth, I would never replace my time in IB for any other learning opportunity. The education program is that good.

Looking forward into the future, I have made up my mind that I will go to university. I am hoping to major in Statistics, and follow my father's footsteps in that field. But, we'll see. The future is always uncertain. Hoping for the best though.


"The unexamined life is not worth living."
