Intro and Books I Like

I have always loved to read and write. To me, the art of language is a great unifier of people. It is the art form that I prefer. As a result, I love to read both fiction and non-fiction books. With that said, I will always prefer fiction. It is my favorite form of escapism. Often, I find that fiction can teach us great truths that even reality will have a tough time teaching us. Given that extended spiel about books, I think it is only right, that I also lise some of the favorite books that I have read over the years.

  1. Crime and Punishment by Fyodor Dostoevsky

  2. War and Peace by Leo Tolstoy
  3. Brave New World by Aldous Huxley
  4. The Divine Comedy by Dante Aligieri
  5. The Harry Potter Series by JK Rowling (yes it's a series, but who doesn't like it?)
