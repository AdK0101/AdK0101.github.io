My Hobbies

History

Studying history is a absolute joy for me. I truly think that history is the truest stories that people can ever tell. History has so many tragic and beautiful stories that truly show the greatness of humanity and civilization. History as well, shows us the darkest and inhumane aspects of humanity. Both aspects are important to our understanding of who we are today.

Putting aside that pretentious spiel, I just like reading about history. Simple as that.


Geography and Society

Just as I love learning about history, I also like learning about current society. There is certainly something interesting about learning about the different traditions that you see around the world. You can learn much about the nature of humanity, from simply analyzing the different ways that humans form and structure people-groups around the world.


Debate

Equally, I have medalled at both on the regional circuit, and at provincials. Twice I have debated on the national level, once in grade 9 and the other in grade 11. In fact, I debated in nationals just a few days ago. Because of COVID-19, we had to do the debate tournament online, which was an experience. I debate currently with my partner, Dawson Puderak.
