Sports I Love

Cricket

Cricket has got to be my favorite sport. I know many people can call it boring to watch and play, but I think that the long nature of the game only adds to the drama. Cricket is one of those few games that truly has the time to really ebb and flow across days.


Soccer

Soccer is also one of my favorite sports to not only watch, but also play. I have been playing soccer since I was young. I have played for the Eastside Soccer club in Saskatoon, and before that, community soccer. Nowadays, I play soccer for my high school team. I primarily play as a defender.


Basketball

Basketball is a sport that I have picked up a liking for relatively recently (by relatively, I mean like 4 years ago). My favorite Canadian basketball team is by default, the Toronto Raptors. Likewise, my favorite American team is the LA Clippers. I've always loved a good underdog story, and basketball is honestly full of them.
